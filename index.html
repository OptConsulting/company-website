About Us

Thoughts, attitudes and behaviors significantly impact our physical health and longevity. Similarly, our diet, sleep patterns and exercise levels impact not only our physical body but also our mental state. The mind and body are deeply connected; physical ailments affect us mentally and emotional stressors weigh on our physical well-being.

At Options Consulting, we strive to treat the whole person, not just their symptoms. Helping each patient achieve their highest level of recovery and participation in life.

We specialize in acute, post-acute, and post-surgical medical management and workers' compensation claim management. We offer medical treatment, care coordination, and behavioral and mental health interventions.

Our Providers

Use of both pharmaceutical and supplemental treatments can be very beneficial to help patients along the path to wellness.
